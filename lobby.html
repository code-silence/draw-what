SYSTEM MANUAL: HOW TO PLAY

1. ESTABLISH A NODE (ROOM):
Create a new room and share the 6-digit Code with your friends, or enter a Code to join an existing room.

2. TRANSMITTER (DRAWER):
When it's your turn, a secret word will appear on your screen. Sketch it on the holographic board! (Writing letters or numbers is strictly prohibited).

3. RECEIVER (GUESSER):
Watch the live transmission of the drawing. Type your guess in the console box below. The faster you guess correctly, the more points you earn!

4. VICTORY:
The operator with the highest sync rate (Points) at the end of all rounds wins the game!
